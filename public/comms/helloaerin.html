What's this about?

helloaerin is an artist I have commissioned a lot in the past and whose work I absoutely adore! I've been ordering art from her since 2024, and all of the icons I use on my character directory are created by her!

I wanted to make this page compiling almost all of the art I've received from them, both for myself as I love to see it all in one place, and also as a showcase and a token of appreciation for an artist I would highly recommend anybody go and commission.

She offers a ton of YCH-style options, different art styles (mainly for chibis), and even mini animations -- all at extremely affordable prices. So, if you're ever looking for some cute art of your favourite little guys, please consider commissioning her! She seriously deserves the support and more!

Find her at some of the links below!
