Backstory

Years in future, Ana -- originally under a different name -- was created for the Dark Continent Broadcasting Company (or "DBC") as a 24-hour entertainer for their television programs. They worked in a DBC studio in the port city of Anchroun. as a production assistant working behind the scenes and helping the late-night performers.

However, when a show host failed to show up for their morning block (due to falling asleep at the studio), Ana made their first on-air appearance filling in for them. Ana's performance was a hit and audiences were excited to see them again. Because of this, Ana was assigned to co-star on a number of programs to try and find a new placement for them that best suited their brand of entertainment.

Ana was popular with the DBC staff, and they made many friends while working at the studio. They bonded with a journalist who wanted to take them overseas with him to visit the Shimmering Coast Resort as part of piece he was working on to review luxury accommodations across the Great Ocean's cities.

During the trip while their friend was occupied, Ana was mugged by gang members who were preying on vulnerable-looking tourists. Being that they did not want to cause trouble as a representative of the DBC, Ana did not retaliate and was savagely beaten once the thugs realized they were a full machine.

Ana's systems shut down and their body was taken away to be salvaged for parts to sell. In the meantime, their journalist friend had no choice but to leave without them after weeks had passed and they were nowhere to be found. Once the thugs realized Ana was likely not of any value in the state that they were in, they were thrown into the trash.

Ana was discovered by Ambrosius and Theo, members of the Global Communication Setup Foundation from the distant past. They were in the area as part of a vacation orchestrated by Tempo the Dialga, a powerful time-traveler.

Pitying the broken machine, the rescuers found and paid a technician in the city to fix Ana's body and restore their systems to a functional state. During the process of being fixed however, Theo asked about an insignia that was attached to Ana's person and he was told that it belonged to a kingdom of the Dark Continent.

Knowing that Axel Fulhaus and Niccolo Bernardi -- fellow members of the GCSF -- were from the war-torn island, Theo informed them of Ana's existence and intended to introduce them to the robot once repairs were completed. Ana was given their current name by Theo while awaiting their revival.

Niccolo and Axel met with Ana a few weeks later. While Axel was ecstatic, Niccolo was desperate to hear from them about the future of the Dark Continent, having escaped the conflict himself. In being restored to working condition however, it turned out that Ana's memory had been damaged and they recalled almost nothing about themself.

After spending a few days together and getting to know each other, Axel invited Ana to come back to Ethovalon and their own time with him and Niccolo. Meanwhile, Niccolo was still desperate for answers and hoped that Ana would be able to regain their memories to tell him about the future of the Dark Continent. With nowhere else to go and with coaxing from Niccolo, Ana decided to accept the invitation.

Given that Niccolo and Axel's team was full at the time, the two asked if Theo would be willing to allow Ana to join his team. It ultimately seemed to be the best choice as Theo's partner -- Hawke -- was well-versed in the upkeep of machinery and would likely be better suited to helping Ana maintain his robotics for the time. Thus, Ana joined team Syzygy.

Despite the loss of memory, much of Ana's core programming remains in place and he has retained much of his previous personality. Though he physically is unable to process much of his past, he still recognizes that there is something missing, locked away, or corrupted, and he is eager to work with both his new team and his Dark Continent friends to attempt to jog his memory. Being unbothered by not knowing what he's missing however, he's been perfectly content to adjust to life in Ethovalon and as a member of the GCSF, opting not to rush his recovery.
