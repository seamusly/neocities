History

Place of Origin

Brightdust, Starscarred Lands

Zane was born in the modest little village of Asterei on the Starry Cascada Islands. His mother Naminea raised him whilst his father Bazylthram was largely missing from his youth. The latter served as a diplomat for the illustrious Starfall City and would often be traveling to other islands, and so there was seldom time for him to visit home. Despite his father's absence, Zane lived a generally happy childhood. As a boy, his mother helped him develop his lifelong love of music and performance, and he aspired to become an adventuring bard one day.

When Zane was around 9 years old, his mother suddenly and mysterious disappeared. For a time, his neighbours cared for him in the hopes that Naminea would return or get into contact with them. However, after weeks had passed with no news, his caretakers ultimately decided to call on Bazylthram to come and retrieve his son. It took several more weeks before Bazylthram arrived back in Asterei. When he left, he took Zane with him back to the city with the intent of leaving behind their family home for good.

As Zane grew into a teenager, he found himself clashing often with his father. Bazylthram was at a loss as to how to raise his son, and so Zane was often left to his own devices or neglected entirely. Around this time, as he was allowed to roam the city at his discretion, he fell in with a band of rogues who called themselves "the Iris". The gang was led by a man named Hisekawa, who seemed to act as a sort of surrogate father to many of the younger members like Zane himself.

Throughout his teenage years and into his early adulthood, Zane stayed with the Iris and forged closed bonds with the various members of the crew. Now with years of experience under their belts, Hisekawa felt they were ready to take on a particularly risky heist with a very generous payout. After extensive planning and preparations, the Iris was able to complete the heist, only to be betrayed by their leader in the aftermath as Hisekawa ran off with the payload and alerted the authorities as to their whereabouts. Following that, the members of the Iris scattered.

By chance, as he had ducked into a skyport to hide, Zane ended up accidentally stowing away on an airship that would soon depart from Starfall City. While hiding amidst the cargo, he was discovered by a person named Gwisin -- a member of a bounty hunting team aboard the ship. Despite seemingly realizing that Zane was a wanted man, Gwisin said nothing of the matter and instead accompanied him for the remainder of the trip. Their destination was the city of Ethovalon on Kala Island, located far below the Starry Cascada Islands in the Great Ocean.

Over the course of the journey, Gwisin informed Zane about the GCSF and its status as an exploration guild. Though Zane could not fully understand their actions, Gwisin extended an invitation to him to join their team, Enochian. Having had it pitched to him as a chance to make money and lay low by traveling the breadth of the Great Ocean for a time, Zane accepted.

Though he now works as member of the bounty hunting branch and largely avoids having to set foot in the Sea of Clouds, Zane ultimately hopes to discover what happened to the other members of the Iris and confront Hisekawa for his betrayal.
