Summary
  • Augurys was born in the distant future, on a floating continent that was torn apart by a meteor called the Starscarred Lands.
  • The islands were covered in cosmic powers and potent magics that drew scholars and mages all across the realm. Augurys followed in the footsteps of their parents and wished to understand and make use of the land's magics.
  • After reaching adulthood, they moved to the city of Solhart to attend Astron Academy.
  • Early into their first year, they met a Malamar named Ephemir. The two quickly became close friends and were practically inseparable before long.
  • At some point after meeting Ephemir, Augurys began to suffer from memory loss and confusion. As a result, they started to rely heavily on Ephemir to help them in their daily life.
  • Towards the end of the academic year, Ephemir invited Augurys to accompany him back to his home: one of the impact sites of the meteor that struck the continent years ago.
  • On the day of their departure, while Augurys was alone and preparing for the trip, a powerful mage broke into their home and urged Augurys to leave with them. They tried to warn Augurys that Ephemir was a dangerous individual who had been hypnotizing them over the last few months.
  • Suddenly, Ephemir returned and clashed with the mage. The ensuing use of chaotic magics caused an eruption that sent Augurys back in time to the present day.
  • Augurys was found in a catatonic state by villagers living in the Land of Mists. After they regained consciousness, the villagers opted to contact the GCSF for aid.
  • Members of the rescuer team Syzygy arrived and took charge of Augurys. While exploring options for treatment in Ethovalon afterwards, Augurys became enamoured by the thought of living as an explorer. They asked to join the team soon after.
  • Though the team's leaders hesitated at first, they ultimately allowed Augurys to join them under the condition that they were frequently monitored so that they wouldn't land themself into trouble.

Backstory

Augurys was born long after the establishment of the Global Communications Setup Foundation. They were raised on a continent located in the Sea of Clouds known as the Starscarred Lands.

The town of Brightdust was their home for most of their life. Brightdust was known primarily for its mining industry though it was also a relatively safe place for interested arcane adepts and scientific researchers to sample some of the magical geological phenomena common across the island and especially near craters. Being that the town had such a close relation to the land's inherent magical nature, Augurys's parents moved there and settled before they were born.

Throughout most of Augurys's childhood, they followed the example of their family members and learned from the brilliant minds of their community. They had an innate curiosity for all things related to magic and the nature of the cosmic powers that had transformed the land. They spent most of their early years learning to channel some of the power themself, honing their ability to wield it in a variety of ways including for offence and for simply day-to-day utility. By the time they were reaching their adulthood, they were eager to continue their studies and move onto new opportunities. To that end, they ventured to Solhart; a major city on the continent and home to the top-rated university across the Starscarred Lands known as the Astron Academy.

Within their first year of attending the academy, Augurys became acquainted with a Malamar named Ephemir who they noticed across the room during one of their lectures. They were compelled to speak with him, taking an interest in his participation in their shared class at the time. The two were immediately friendly with one another and before long, Augurys found him to be a constant companion of theirs throughout the academic year.

Some time after Augurys had met Ephemir however, they found themself much more scatterbrained way than usual. They began to struggle with their memory as well, usually forgetting the details of their day and eventually being unable to recall where they were the night prior. During this time, Ephemir graciously offered his aid. Given that they were together so often, he would fill in the blanks for Augurys and help them with their day-to-day activities. After a while, it became that Ephemir was a part of almost every aspect of Augurys's life, and they were seemingly content to be with him.

One day, Ephemir spoke of a place he considered home: a section of impact site where the great meteor had struck the continent centuries ago, and one of the scattered origin points of the cosmic powers of the Starscarred Lands. He invited Augurys to come with him to visit the crater some time in the near future, and Augurys happily accepted. They planned to take the trip once the academic year had concluded.

Augurys was so eager to see such a historical site that the remainder of the school year passed quickly. On the day that they were to depart Solhart with Ephemir, they were left alone for a brief period of time while Ephemir was doing one last errand run. During that time, a person broke into their home and restrained them in a hurry. Augurys realized that they were a magic-user and one that was decorated in icons reserved only for experts in the arcane arts within the city at that. The mage urged Augurys to come quietly and told them that Ephemir was unstable and a threat to both their safety and the city at large as he had been rewriting their personality for his personal gain since they met.

Before Augurys could question the mage however, Ephemir arrived and a battle broke out. In the ensuing conflict, as the mage and Ephemir clashed and filled the air with chaotic magics, there was an explosion and a bright light that consumed them all.

When Augurys next came to consciousness, they were being cared for by a pair strangers in an unfamiliar village. The couple informed them that a group of children had ventured to the outskirts of the village and found them alone in a catatonic state within the forest. It had been a few days since the incident, and the couple had been caring for them since. Augurys was unable to answer questions about their past, who they were, or where they came from, rendering them a complete amnesiac in a foreign land. They were later told that they were in the Land of Mists and despite their seemingly troubled past, they were fairly content and even cheerful in the days that followed.

The villagers opted to call on an exploration guild for aid with their dilemma and allowed Augurys to stay with them until help arrived. Within a few days, members of a GCSF rescue team arrived in the village. Among them were Theo and Ambrosius, the leaders of a team called Syzygy. The two were happy to take Augurys back to the guild's base of operations on Kala Island with the hopes of helping them regain their memories.

Soon after arriving at Ethovalon and after spending time with the members of Syzygy, Augurys became endeared to the team and the idea of life with an exploration guild. They asked if there was a way for them to register to become a part of the team, and were met with hesitation from the leaders. Both Theo and Ambrosius as well as Hawke -- who served as an additional authority on the team -- were concerned with Augurys's overall disposition. They were extremely well-read and knowledgeable on subjects beyond most of the team's members, yet their curiosity and lack of self-preservation was sure to land them in trouble. Knowing this, and realizing that they could potentially end up in an even more precarious profession, the leaders of Syzygy agreed to accept them as a new recruit and have since been monitoring them closely.
